# Quick Start

1. Run `run_windows.bat` or `python server.py`.
2. Leave **Guided learning** enabled.
3. Open **Assessment campaign** and identify the exact APIM, GWM, and TCU software baseline.
4. Open **Applicability matrix** and read why the GWM diagnostic-isolation requirement applies.
5. Open **Evidence request plan** and compare the requested evidence with what was received.
6. Open **Control evaluation** and review the seeded GWM result:
   - design effective;
   - implemented;
   - operating effectiveness partially effective;
   - overall partially met.
7. Inspect `EVD-TCU-001`, `EVD-GWM-001`, and `EVD-LOG-001` in **Evidence library**.
8. Open **Findings & actions** to follow the gap through root cause, remediation, retest, and approval.
9. Complete the **Audit defense** questions without revealing expected points.
10. Use **Interview walkthrough** for a five-minute demonstration.

After one guided run, reset the database and repeat in **Analyst mode** before asking the local assistant.
