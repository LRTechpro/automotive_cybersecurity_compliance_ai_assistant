Core lesson

Compliance conclusions have a scope and shelf life

A passing test for TCU 5.2 does not automatically support TCU 5.3. Reuse is defensible only when the relevant design, software, configuration, interfaces, and operating conditions remain unchanged.

  • A component can invalidate another component’s evidence through an interface change.
  • An SBOM identifies ingredients; it does not approve the release.
  • A mapping supports traceability; it does not automatically prove conformance.
  • The tool calculates posture; an authorized person decides.

Decision rule

When should evidence become stale?

Mark evidence stale or require retest when a relevant implementation, dependency, configuration, interface, test environment, requirement, or threat assumption changes. Document the reason rather than simply expiring everything by date.
